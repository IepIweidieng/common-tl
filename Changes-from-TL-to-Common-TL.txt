Changes from TL to Common TL, revision 6

CTL.: Common TL phonetic notation system
N.: Common TL phonetic for north dialect (in Common TL)
S.: Common TL phonetic for south dialect (in Common TL)
TL.: Taiwanese Romanization System
To.: Taiwanese Hokkien Choân-chiu accent (in TL)
Ci.: Taiwanese Hokkien Chiang-chiu accent (in TL)
M.: Modern Standard Mandarin Chinese (in Zhuyin)
Hk.: Taiwanese Hakka (in THRS)
